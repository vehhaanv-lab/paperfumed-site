Privacy Notice

Last updated: April 2026

PaperFumed ("we", "us") is a Bengaluru-based early-stage company building paper-based packaging for India. This notice explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it.

What we collect

When you submit the early access form on our homepage, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your company or organisation name
  • The reason for your enquiry (selected from a dropdown)
  • Your message

We do not collect your email address, phone number, or any payment information through this site.

Why we collect it

The information you submit is used solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not use it for marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making.

Who processes it

Form submissions are routed through FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), a US-based form handling service, before being delivered to our inbox. FormSubmit processes your submission in transit; it does not retain your data beyond delivery. You can review FormSubmit's privacy policy at formsubmit.co.

How long we keep it

We retain your submitted information in our email inbox for as long as it is relevant to our correspondence with you, typically no longer than 12 months from the date of submission.

Your rights under DPDPA 2023

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Withdraw consent (by requesting deletion)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address below.

Cookies

This site does not set first-party cookies. FormSubmit may set a session cookie during form submission for spam prevention purposes; this cookie is not used for tracking or advertising. We do not use analytics tools on this site at this time.
