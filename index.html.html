India is the world's largest plastic polluter — generating 9.3–10.2 million tonnes of plastic waste annually, accounting for nearly one-fifth of global emissions.

Why Paper

Built from renewable
materials. Designed
for circularity.

Paper-based bottles are made from FSC-certified wood fibre or sugarcane pulp with a food-safe biodegradable lining. They hold water effectively, ship like standard bottles, and decompose without leaving a trace.

0% Plastic in the bottle construction. The barrier lining is bio-based.
2–6 Weeks to fully decompose, versus 800+ years for a PET plastic bottle.
70% Reduction in plastic content versus a standard PET bottle (Paboco / industry benchmark).
100% Recyclable within the paper stream — no specialist facility required.
