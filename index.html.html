The Science

Paper is not a
compromise. It is the answer.

2–6 wk

Paper decomposes completely

Under natural conditions, a paper-based bottle fully biodegrades in two to six weeks. PET plastic takes four hundred to eight hundred years and then fragments into persistent microplastics.

Decomposition research consensus
68 vs 9%

Paper is actually recycled

The global paper recycling rate is 68%. The rate for plastic is 9%. Paper is recycled at more than seven times the efficiency of plastic using infrastructure already in place.

UNEP Plastics Report  ·  FAO Paper Data
60%

Lower carbon footprint

Paper-based bottles produce an estimated 60% lower carbon footprint per unit versus virgin PET manufacturing, based on full lifecycle analysis including fiber sourcing and forming.

Paboco LCA  ·  Industry lifecycle benchmarks
0g

Zero microplastic residue

Paper bottles with bio-based coatings decompose without leaving microplastic residue. PET fragments into particles that persist indefinitely in soil, water systems, and human tissue.

Nature Medicine 2025  ·  Materials science
FSC

Renewable by definition

Paper bottles are made from FSC-certified wood fiber or agricultural byproducts such as sugarcane pulp. Both are genuinely renewable, replacing fossil-derived petrochemical feedstocks.

Forest Stewardship Council
2022

India's ban is already active

India banned 19 single-use plastic categories in July 2022. EPR compliance requirements escalate through 2029. Large PET bottles must reach 85% reuse rates under current law.

Plastic Waste Management Rules  ·  MOEFCC
