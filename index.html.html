The Science

Paper is not a compromise.
It is the answer.

2–6 wk

Paper decomposes completely

Under natural conditions a paper-based bottle biodegrades in two to six weeks. PET plastic takes four to eight hundred years and fragments into microplastics that persist indefinitely.

Global decomposition research consensus
68 vs 9%

Paper is actually recycled

The global paper recycling rate is 68%. The rate for plastic is 9%. Paper is recycled at more than seven times the real-world efficiency of plastic using infrastructure already in place.

UNEP Plastics Report · FAO Paper Data
85%

Paper construction

Leading paper bottles are already 85% paper with a thin bio-based barrier lining. The industry roadmap targets a fully plastic-free construction. PaperFumed is building toward that vision.

Paboco Next Gen Paper Bottle · 2024
60%

Lower carbon footprint

Paper-based bottles produce an estimated 60% lower carbon footprint per unit versus virgin PET manufacturing, based on full lifecycle analysis including fiber sourcing, forming, and end-of-life.

Industry lifecycle analysis benchmarks
FSC

Renewable by definition

Paper bottles use FSC-certified wood fiber or agricultural byproducts such as sugarcane pulp. Both are genuinely renewable, replacing fossil-derived petrochemical feedstocks entirely for the outer shell.

Forest Stewardship Council
2022

India's ban is already active

India banned 19 categories of single-use plastic in July 2022. EPR compliance mandates escalate through 2029. Businesses that don't adapt will face growing regulatory and reputational risk.

Plastic Waste Management Rules 2021 · MOEFCC
