The Science

Paper is not a compromise.
It is the answer.

Six facts. Each sourced. Paper wins on every dimension that matters for packaging at scale.

2–6 wk

Paper decomposes completely

Under natural conditions, a paper-based bottle biodegrades in two to six weeks (under industrial composting conditions). A PET plastic bottle takes up to 450 years and then fragments into microplastics that persist indefinitely in soil, water, and human tissue.

CSIRO · WWF · US National Park Service
60 vs 9%

Paper is actually recycled

The global paper recycling rate is approximately 60%. The rate for plastic is just 9% and has not crossed double digits globally. Paper is recycled at more than six times the efficiency of plastic using infrastructure already in place worldwide.

CEPI Paper Recycling Report 2026 · Science Advances
85%

Paper construction today

Leading paper bottles are already 85% paper with a bio-based barrier lining weighing under 2 grams. The industry roadmap targets fully bio-based construction. PaperFumed is building toward that future.

Paboco Next Gen Paper Bottle · 2026
60%

Lower carbon in production

Paper-based bottles are estimated to produce a significantly lower carbon footprint per unit versus virgin PET manufacturing when accounting for renewable fiber sourcing and end-of-life biodegradation.

Industry lifecycle analysis benchmarks · Paboco LCA data
FSC

Renewable by definition

Paper bottles are made from FSC-certified wood fiber or agricultural byproducts such as sugarcane pulp. Both are genuinely renewable, replacing fossil-derived petrochemical feedstocks for the outer shell entirely.

Forest Stewardship Council
2022

India's ban is already active

India banned 19 categories of single-use plastic in July 2022. EPR compliance mandates escalate through 2029. Businesses that do not adapt will face growing regulatory and reputational risk.

Plastic Waste Management Rules 2021 · MOEFCC · PIB India
